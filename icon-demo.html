Stod Brick Mascot

Extended expressions based on the V1 design.

Pensive
Default idle state. Calm, observant, ready to learn.
Kinetic
Falls, squashes, recovers. For loading states.
Zen
Floats peacefully. For success or completion.
Pondering
Wobbles while thinking. Eyes drift upward.
Reading
Eyes scan across. For active study mode.
Delighted
Happy squint, big smile. For achievements.
Confused
Crooked glasses, wavy mouth. For tough problems.
Shocked
Raised glasses, open mouth. For errors or surprises.
Sleeping
Eyes closed, Zs floating. For offline or idle.
Confident
Sunglasses, cool demeanor. For mastery/expertise.
Stressed
Sweat drops, shaking. For deadlines/errors.
Determined
Furrowed brows, focused. For hard challenges.
Favicon
Zoomed in with thicker strokes. Optimized for 16x16 / 32x32.
