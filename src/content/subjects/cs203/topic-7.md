## Core Concepts

Computational complexity classifies problems by the resources (time, space) needed to solve them. It reveals deep structure in the landscape of computational problems.

### Complexity Classes

P contains problems solvable in polynomial time. NP contains problems verifiable in polynomial time. The P vs NP question asks whether verification implies efficient solution.
